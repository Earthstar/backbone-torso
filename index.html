What is Backbone-torso?

Backbone provides many convenient tools for building Single Page Applications (SPAs) out of the box, and we absolutely love using it! However, the bare Backbone library could use some love in areas such as response forms, data validation tools, polling collections, and sub-view management. Beyond Torso's handy-dandy utility classes, there are some opinionated design philosophies that we've adopted to make reliable and maintainable Backbone apps.
